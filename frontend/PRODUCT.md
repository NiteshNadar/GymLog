# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Gym-goers and fitness enthusiasts tracking their workouts on mobile devices. They need to log exercises, weights, reps, and sets quickly and easily during their active training sessions.

## Product Purpose
Gym Log 2.0 exists to provide a speed-optimized, premium, and frictionless mobile tracking experience that helps users record their workouts without interrupting their training flow.

## Brand Personality
- **Linear/Raycast inspired**: Dark, sophisticated, technical, and high-performance.
- **Minimalist & Precise**: Clean lines, subtle borders, high contrast, and zero clutter.
- **Reliable & Fast**: Trustworthy interactions, rapid input transitions, and smooth micro-animations.

## Anti-references
- Overly bright, neon-drenched "crypto" styles that cause eye strain in dim gym environments.
- SaaS-cream or light-mode-first interfaces that look like spreadsheets or office work.
- Nested cards and redundant containers that reduce the scrollable workspace.
- Clunky, multi-step forms that delay set logging.

## Design Principles
1. **Zero-friction Logging**: Optimize the interface for single-hand, rapid logging. Inputs should auto-focus, and suggestions should be easily accessible.
2. **High-Performance Dark Theme**: Use a rich, low-light visual system with subtle borders and clear contrast to ensure legibility under gym lighting conditions.
3. **Micro-interactivity**: Provide clear visual feedback (e.g., transitions, hover and active states) for successful database interactions and logged sets without intrusive alerts.
4. **Mobile-first App Shell**: The layout must feel like a native mobile app container, utilizing sticky headers/footers and smooth touch-optimized zones.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High color contrast (WCAG AA compliant) for readability under harsh overhead lights or sweat-fogged displays.
- Touch targets of at least 44x44px for easy input.
- Respect user preference for reduced motion.
